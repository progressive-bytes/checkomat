PRIVACY POLICY

CHECKOMAT

Effective Date: June 1, 2025
Last Updated: January 1, 2026

OVERVIEW

Progressive Bytes ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Checkomat mobile application (the "Service").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We do not collect any personal information.

Checkomat is designed to work entirely on your device without requiring any personal data collection.

Data Stored Locally:

  • Checklist Data: All checklists, items, and related data you create are stored locally on your device.
  • App Preferences: Settings and preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • No Cloud Storage: Your data is never uploaded to our servers or any cloud services.

HOW WE USE INFORMATION

Since we don't collect any personal information, we don't use your personal data in any way.

All app functionality operates entirely on your device.

DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

We do not share any data.

Because all data remains on your device and we don't collect personal information:

  • We don't share data with third parties.
  • We don't sell your information.
  • We don't use your data for advertising.
  • We don't analyze your usage patterns.

DATA SECURITY

Your checklist data is stored securely on your device using standard iOS security measures.

  • Data is protected by your device's built-in security features.
  • No data transmission occurs over networks.
  • You maintain complete control over your data.

DATA RETENTION AND DELETION

  • Local Storage: Your checklist data remains on your device until you delete the app or manually delete the data.
  • App Deletion: When you delete the app, all associated data is permanently removed from your device.
  • Manual Deletion: You can delete individual or all checklists and items at any time within the app.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our Service is suitable for users of all ages.

Since we don't collect any personal information, there are no special considerations for users under 13 years of age.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Checkomat does not integrate with any third-party services that collect personal information.

ANALYTICS AND CRASH REPORTING

We do not use any analytics or crash reporting services that collect personal information.

ADVERTISING

Checkomat does not display advertisements.

CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this webpage.
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of the Privacy Policy.
  • If significant changes are made, we may provide additional notice through the app or other means.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

YOUR RIGHTS

Since we don't collect personal data, traditional data protection rights (access, correction, deletion) don't apply.

However, you have complete control over your data:

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app.
  • Modification: You can edit or update your checklists and items at any time.
  • Deletion: You can delete items, checklists, or all data by deleting the app.

CONTACT US

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: contact@progressive-bytes.com
  • App Store: Through the Checkomat app page review section.

COMPLIANCE

This privacy policy is designed to comply with:

  • Apple's App Store Review Guidelines.
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

SUMMARY

Checkomat is a privacy-friendly app that stores all your checklist data locally on your device.

We don't collect, store, or share any personal information.

Your data stays on your device and under your complete control.
